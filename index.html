Ordinal XXX




Ordinal XXX is a collection of 50 pornstars incribed on the Bitcoin blockchain. The impact of technology on sex and pornography has been significant, altering the way we engage with sexuality and explicit content. Using the ordinal theory, this collection of minimalistic sexual figures offers an artistic portrayal of the intersection between pornography, Bitcoin blockchain, and technology.
